Cuban Ropa Vieja

This ropa vieja is great served on tortillas or over rice. Add sour cream, cheese, and fresh cilantro on the side.

Ingredients

    1 tablespoon vegetable oil 2 pounds beef flank steak 1 cup beef broth 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste 1 green bell pepper, seeded and sliced into strips 1 small onion, sliced 2 cloves garlic, chopped 1 teaspoon ground cumin 1 teaspoon chopped fresh cilantro 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 tablespoon white vinegar
